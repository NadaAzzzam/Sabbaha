## Description

Habbah (حَبّة) — meaning "bead" in Arabic — is a beautifully minimal dhikr counter designed for Muslims who want to be intentional about their daily remembrance of Allah.

SMART COUNTING
Tap anywhere on screen to count. No tiny buttons — the entire screen is your counter. Choose fixed count mode with a visual progress ring, or free count mode for unlimited dhikr. Feel each bead through gentle haptic feedback and soft audio cues.

TRACK YOUR JOURNEY
Build daily streaks to stay consistent. View detailed per-dhikr statistics, interactive weekly and monthly charts, and see how much time you've spent in remembrance. Your progress motivates your practice.

BEAUTIFUL & FOCUSED
Designed with deep forest green and gold — colors inspired by Islamic art. Dark and light modes, smooth Reanimated transitions, and a clean interface free from distractions.

FULLY CUSTOMIZABLE
Add unlimited custom dhikr. Adjust haptic intensity, sound volume, and set gentle reminders. Switch between Arabic and English instantly. Habbah adapts to how you worship.

PRIVACY FIRST
All data stays on your device. No account, no ads, no tracking, no data collection. Works completely offline.

DEFAULT DHIKR INCLUDED
Six authentic adhkar from Sahih Bukhari and Muslim — SubhanAllah, Alhamdulillah, Allahu Akbar, La ilaha illallah, SubhanAllahi wa bihamdihi, and Astaghfirullah.

Free and ad-free, made with love for the Ummah.
