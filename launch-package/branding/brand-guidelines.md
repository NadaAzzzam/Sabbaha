# Habbah (حَبّة) Brand Guidelines

## Name & Meaning
- **App Name:** Habbah (حَبّة)
- **Meaning:** "Habbah" (حَبّة) means "grain" or "bead" in Arabic — referring to a single bead of a tasbih (prayer beads). It symbolizes the simplicity and weight of each moment spent in remembrance of Allah.
- **Package Name:** `com.sabbaha.app` (derived from "سَبَّحَ" — to glorify/praise Allah)
- **Tagline (EN):** "Feel every bead"
- **Tagline (AR):** "اشعر بكل حبّة"

## Logo Concept
The logo features a single golden bead (the "habbah") centered on a deep forest green background. A subtle dashed ring around the bead suggests the tasbih string without being literal. The bead has a soft 3D quality with a highlight and a small string hole at its center. The Arabic calligraphy "حَبّة" appears below the bead.

**Why this works:**
- The single bead is the core metaphor — each dhikr counts, each moment matters
- Gold on deep green is a classic Islamic color pairing (prestige + tranquility)
- The design is minimal and scalable — works at 16px favicon and 1024px app icon
- No direct similarity to any existing dhikr app icon (most use crescent moons, mosques, or full prayer bead circles)

## Uniqueness Validation
- **Google Play Store:** No app named "Habbah" or "حَبّة" found (searched April 2026)
- **Apple App Store:** No app named "Habbah" or "حَبّة" found (searched April 2026)
- **Trademark:** "Habbah" is a common Arabic word meaning "grain/bead" — low trademark conflict risk for an app name. No registered software trademarks found for this term.
- **Differentiators vs competitors:**
  - Most dhikr apps use mosque/crescent/full-rosary icons — Habbah uses a single bead
  - Minimalist approach vs feature-heavy competitors
  - Session-based with time tracking and streaks (gamification)
  - Gold-on-green branding is distinctive in the category
