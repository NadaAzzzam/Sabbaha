# Privacy Policy — Habbah (حَبّة)

**Last updated:** April 13, 2026

## Introduction

Habbah ("we", "our", or "the app") is a dhikr (Islamic remembrance) counter application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

Habbah is designed with privacy as a core principle. The app:

- Does **not** require an account or registration
- Does **not** collect personal data (name, email, phone number, etc.)
- Does **not** track your location
- Does **not** use analytics or tracking tools
- Does **not** contain advertisements
- Does **not** use cookies
- Does **not** transmit any data over the internet

## Data Stored Locally

Habbah stores the following data **exclusively on your device**:

| Data | Purpose | Storage |
|------|---------|---------|
| Dhikr session history | Display statistics and charts | Device local storage (MMKV) |
| Custom dhikr entries | Allow personalized dhikr list | Device local storage (MMKV) |
| App preferences | Remember your settings (theme, language, haptics, sound) | Device local storage (MMKV) |
| Active session state | Resume session if app is closed | Device local storage (MMKV) |

This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not backed up to any server
- Is not shared with any third party
- Can be deleted at any time through the app's Settings (Clear History)
- Is automatically removed when you uninstall the app

## Third-Party Services

Habbah does **not** integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data. The app functions entirely offline.

## Permissions

Habbah may request the following device permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | Optional? |
|------------|---------|-----------|
| Vibration | Provide haptic feedback during dhikr counting | Used automatically; can be disabled in Settings |
| Notifications | Send optional dhikr reminders | Yes — only if you enable reminders in Settings |
| Exact Alarm | Schedule precise reminder notifications | Yes — only used with reminders feature |

No permission is used to collect, transmit, or process personal data.

## Children's Privacy

Habbah does not collect any data from any user, including children. The app contains no objectionable content, no ads, no in-app purchases, and no social features. It is suitable for users of all ages.

## Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, data security depends on your device's own security measures (passcode, biometric lock, encryption). We recommend keeping your device software up to date.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time:
1. **Within the app:** Settings → Clear History (deletes session history)
2. **Complete removal:** Uninstall the app (removes all stored data including preferences)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and similar regulations, you have the right to:
- **Access** your data — all data is visible within the app
- **Delete** your data — use Clear History in Settings or uninstall the app
- **Port** your data — data is stored locally and accessible on your device
- **Restrict processing** — we do not process any data on servers

Since we collect no personal data and have no servers, these rights are inherently fulfilled.
